Our Philosophy

Table Football is classic and well enjoyed game, but with unlocked potential. This simple but exciting game introduces features to enhance the enjoyment of Table Football.

You can now customise players and formations, adapt the game difficultly via the pitch tilt, and enjoy the convenience of automatic ball return and scoring!

Get Started!

Four simple additions to make a world of difference

Adaptive Difficulty Mode

Motorized legs allow the tilt of the playing surface to be adjusted. If you are too good for your opponent, you can try playing up-hill!

Customizable Formations

Introduce your own tactics into the game by selecting your favoured formation. Players are removable and interchangeable, allowing for enhanced customization

Ball Freeing Mechanism

The days of lifting the table to free the ball are over. Simply press a button and watch the legs move, jolting the ball back into play

Automatic Scoring & Ball Return

The electronic scoreboard will keep count for you automatically, whilst the ball will return to the spring-launcher via a system of rails.
